Who are these cute cats?

These are my household cats, Opal, Loki and Leo. To be accurate, none of them are technically my cats; Opal is my girlfriend's cat, and Loki and Leo are my roommates' cats. However, I love them like my own. They all have very diverse and strong personalities, and although they each have their own idiosyncracies, I wouldn't change any of them for the world.

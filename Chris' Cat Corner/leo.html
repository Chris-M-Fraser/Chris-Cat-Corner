Leo, "The Chonker"

Leo is a 6 year old Tabby. He's a very skittish cat, and he always has a worried look on his face. His defining quality is that he is a bit chubby, but he has lost a lot of weight over the past few months. We usually call him by his nickname, "Big Chonk," or "Chonkers."

Loki and Leo are basically best friends. Leo is actually Loki's uncle, from the same litter as his mother. They love to cuddle and play together, and are essentially unseparable.

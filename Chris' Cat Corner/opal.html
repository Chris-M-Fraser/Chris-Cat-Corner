Opal, "The Princess"

Opal is an 8 year old Maine Coon Tortoiseshell mix. She is highly intelligent, with a somewhat skittish temperament, and doesn't always get along with people or cats, except for my girlfriend who she absolutely loves. She can be a bit of a diva and a drama queen, especially in front of other people or cats, but if you can find her alone, she becomes the sweetest cat ever.
